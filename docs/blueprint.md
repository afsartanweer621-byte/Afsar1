# **App Name**: Mochibazar.com

## Core Features:

- Retailer Onboarding & Verification: Streamlined retailer registration with document upload and backend approval workflow, ensuring only verified retailers access the platform.
- Product Catalog & Search: Comprehensive product catalog browsing with category-based display, advanced search functionality, and detailed product information.
- Order Management: Order placement, tracking, and management with support for multiple payment modes and seller-level MOQ rules.
- Credit Line Management: Management of retailer credit lines, including approval workflows, tracking outstanding balances, and automated blocking if the credit limit is exceeded.
- Personalized Product Recommendations: AI-powered product recommendation tool for retailers, based on past purchases and browsing history.
- Seller Management: Functionality to manage seller accounts
- Logistics Controls: Configuration for MOQ rules and return approval workflows.
- Back end admin panel: With inventory management rules.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#1A237E) to convey trust, security, and professionalism, reflecting the B2B nature of the application.
- Background color: Light blue-gray (#E8EAF6), offering a clean and neutral backdrop that ensures readability and reduces visual fatigue.
- Accent color: Gold (#FFD700) is used sparingly for important calls to action (CTAs) and highlights, adding a touch of sophistication.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif known for its modern and neutral aesthetic, is recommended for both headlines and body text to ensure a clean and consistent reading experience.
- Simple, clear icons for navigation and key actions, ensuring intuitive use on both mobile and web platforms.
- A clean, category-based browsing interface with a mobile-first, responsive design ensuring a seamless user experience across devices.
- Subtle transition animations and loading indicators provide visual feedback and enhance the overall user experience without being distracting.
